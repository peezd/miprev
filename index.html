Intro

Heya, I'm Micah. Welcome to my little corner of the internet.

I'm an avid exercise and outdoors enthusiast as well as an accomplished Product management leader. I fell into product management in the 00s, and developed a passion for understanding how to build amazing products that delight customers. Primary specialization in 0-1, Platform, and Technical product management areas, covering B2B and B2C across a bunch of industries and domains.
I've been lucky enough to work with some amazing architects and engineers throughout my career that have helped me along and I've realized the people you work with matter more than just about anything else. I'm also passionate about ESG and social impact, and happiest working in mission-driven organizations.

I'm proud to have served as a Peace Corps volunteer to Ukraine in an economic development program. I'm currently in Colorado splitting time between Denver and the high country of Summit County. Depending on time of year, I can be found out ski touring, climbing, mountain biking, paddleboarding or hiking with our two trouble-making border collies.

To reach me, you can email me at micah.evans at protonmail.com, or reach out to me on LinkedIn or Github.

Career

I'm a product leader with a track record of building high-performing product teams and leading organizational transformations to product-driven strategy and delivery. Change management leader who creates a shared vision and strong culture of respect and trust. Excel in mission-driven startups overseeing ideation through delivery for innovative software products; domain experience in Fintech, e-Commerce, Marketplace, Telecom, SaaS and more across B2B, B2B2C, and B2C products.

I've consistently been identified as an exceptional performer and recognized as the go-to resource to get things done throughout my career, adept and comfortable transitioning between org levels, from building and delivering a strategic 5 year plan to board members, to engaging with customers directly for input, to peering with exceptional engineers to track down that impossible to trace bug.

I also have an interest in how companies can leverage technology to address core societal problems (such as income equality or climate change), and am a strong proponent of companies that identify corporate social responsibility as a core part of their business objectives.

Professional Experience

VP of Product at Atlas Primary: 2022 - 2023

Delivered product strategy for Atlas Primary, a startup in the medical market research industry. Responsibilities included product leadership, building a technical delivery team, and transitioning development in-house.

Chief Technology Officer at CREDO Mobile: 2017 - 2021

Product and technology leadership for CREDO Mobile, an SF Bay area mission-driven mobile phone company. Led digital and product transformation, built top-tier technical teams, developed roadmap and spearheaded technical innovation. Served as Sr. Director of Product and VP of Product and Software Engineering prior to promotion to CTO in early 2020.

  • Led product management, UX/UI, software engineering, data management, IT, and operations; scaled team to 55 members with responsibility for $12 million in budget and roadmap generating ~$100 million in annual revenue
  • Introduced and led company-wide product planning and roadmapping, balancing new product development with support and enhancements for existing products, creating alignment around strategic priorities to deliver growth
  • Reversed declining trends in customer count and profitability in core mobile business, exceeding targets for customer acquisition while improving margin per customer by more than 20%
  • Vision for new vertical expansion, including CREDO Energy renewable energy line launch to 19 markets
  • Increased sales conversion by 20% through new features and personalization of content unlocked via revamped eCommerce experience on Adobe Commerce and launch of Customer Data Platform solution
  • Enhanced reliability and reduced spend by replatforming custom software platform onto modern tech stacks; Migrated infrastructure to cloud in AWS, CRM to Zendesk, Genesys, and CallMiner NLP solution stack, integrated Twilio and Cordial for notifications, WordPress for content marketing, Authorize.net for payments, and more

Director of Product Management at ClickBank: 2014 - 2017

Led product management at ClickBank, a leading global eCommerce retailer and affiliate marketplace providing end-to-end commerce solutions for product creators, marketers, and entrepreneurs.

  • Cultivated and lead high performing product organization with 6 direct reports, overseeing expertise across product management, PMO, technical writing, and product marketing areas
  • Promoted rapidly through positions of increasingly responsibility based on strong financial, operational, and leadership performance; from Product Mgr. to Sr. Product Mgr. to Director within 14 months
  • Craft and deliver product planning and roadmaps, directing strategic growth of the organization
  • Delivery of product strategy presentations to both internal and external stakeholders, including in-person presentation on strategy to the Board of Directors and conference event presentations to audiences of current and prospective clients in audiences of hundreds

Technical Project Manager at CacheMatrix Holdings: 2011 - 2014

Oversaw project delivery and client management for CacheMatrix, a premier Fintech SaaS firm providing solutions for cash management and liquidity to many of the world's largest financial institutions.

  • Project leadership, overseeing creation and maintenance of financial trading platforms for institutional clients, successfully delivering several multi-million dollar projects per year
  • Product management of new product development, serving as primary driver for delivery of market-leading new product offerings for the institutional banking financial sector
  • Served as relationship manager to clients representing 50% of incoming revenue, consistently achieving target levels of year over year revenue growth

Product Manager at IHS Markit: 2010 - 2011

Led product development for sophisticated integrated financial data solutions for IHS Markit Digital, A fintech company that designs, develops and hosts custom websites, reports and tools for the financial industry.

  • Led 20 plus member cross-functional teams to create industry-leading financial research websites and applications for desktop and mobile platforms including iPhone, Android, and Blackberry
  • Managed a portfolio including two Fortune Global 500 financial companies, actively overseeing and growing $1.5 million in annual service-related revenue
  • Maintained full workload within 60 days of employment and was recognized by senior leadership for excellence in performance during first 3 months with the company

Product Manager at Research Now: 2007 - 2010

First product manager for Research Now, the global leader in market research online sampling and data collection. Product delivery for internal platforms and tools, cross-functional team leadership and business process definition.

  • Served as company’s first product manager overseeing capstone product, a Salesforce.com, Savvion BusinessManager, and PeopleSoft integration project, with a successful 500+ employee rollout
  • Managed product lifecycle for Adobe Flex database query tool; successful adoption drove a measurable 40% increase in productivity, creating an annual savings of $1.2m in operational costs
