Intro

Heya, I'm Micah. Welcome to my little corner of the internet.

I'm an accomplished Product management and technology leader. I fell into product management in the 00s, and worked with some amazing teammates and mentors that helped me develop a passion for understanding how to build amazing products that delight customers. Primary specialization in 0-1, Platform, and Technical product management areas, covering B2B and B2C across a bunch of industries and domains.

I've been lucky enough to work with some amazing architects and engineers throughout my career that have helped me along and I've realized the people you work with matter more than just about anything else. I'm also passionate about ESG and social impact, and happiest working in mission-driven organizations.

I'm also an avid exercise and outdoors enthusiast, and in a previous life I'm proud to have served as a Peace Corps volunteer to Ukraine in an economic development program. I'm currently in Colorado and can usually be found in Denver or the high country of Summit County. Depending on time of year, I can be found out ski touring, climbing, mountain biking, paddleboarding or hiking with our two trouble-making border collies.

To reach me, you can email me at micah.evans@pm.me or find me on LinkedIn or Github.
