Career

I'm a seasoned technology leader and product strategist with experience across multiple industries including Fintech, eCommerce, business services, and Telecommunications; specializing in building high performing teams and getting out of their way to empower them to deliver their best work. I also have an interest in how companies can leverage technology to address core societal problems (such as income equality), and am a strong proponent of companies that identify corporate social responsibility as a core part of their business objectives.

I'm also passionate about driving business and product strategy where market problems intersect with company's capabilities and core competencies, crafting future focused strategies with tangible implementation plans to get there. I've consistently been identified as an exceptional performer and recognized as the go-to resource to get things done throughout my career, and am incredibly comfortable with working at all levels, from overseeing and recommending a strategic 5 year plan to engaging with customers directly for input and digging in wherever we need help to move forward.

  • Business: Product Management, Product and Department Budgeting & P&L, Contract Negotiation and Vendor Management, Program Management, Process Improvements, Business Analysis, Legal and Regulatory Compliance (including PCI, CPNI, GDPR, CCPA), BC/DR and Risk Mitigation Planning
